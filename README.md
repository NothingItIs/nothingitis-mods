# NothingItIs Mods

Small, focused Minecraft mods that solve specific gameplay and modpack problems.

## Mods

- **[BredExempt](bredexempt/)** — bred animals and egg-hatched chicks no longer count toward the mob cap, so your farms stop choking natural spawns. Full source in [`bredexempt/source/`](bredexempt/source/). Download on [Modrinth](https://modrinth.com/mod/bredexempt) or [CurseForge](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/bredexempt).
- **[Village Doctor](villagedoctor/)** — right-click a villager with a Stethoscope for a live checkup and a plain diagnosis of why it won't breed, restock, or claim a bed. Full source in [`villagedoctor/source/`](villagedoctor/source/). Download on [Modrinth](https://modrinth.com/mod/villagedoctor) or [CurseForge](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/village-doctor).
